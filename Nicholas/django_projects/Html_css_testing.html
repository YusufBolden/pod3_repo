Family

I value family for a multitude of reasons.

Why I Value financial freedom/independence.

  • Financial independence puts me in control of my own life
  • Financial freedom means being able to help those you care for

The importance of time

  • Time is important because it is the most valuable resource one possess.
  • You can never get or earn it back once it is spent.
  • So you always need to spend it wisely or risk squandering it on the wrong things or people.
